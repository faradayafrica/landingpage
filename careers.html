What we Offer

Faraday doesn't have a big team. At the time of writing this, there's just eight(8) members including the founders and other volunteers working tirelessly with no external funding to see this project to the end. What we do have is a dream, and a family. All we have at the moment to offer is an inclusive environment for your talent to shine and an opportunity to grow while building a real life solution with the family.

These are the positions we want to fill. You don't have to be the best. What we're interested in is your will to solve difficult problems and a spirit of teamwork.
